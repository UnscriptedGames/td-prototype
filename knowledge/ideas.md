# Pending Ideas and Open Questions

**Version:** 0.1 (Living Document)
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-26

This document tracks all pending ideas, brainstorms, and open design questions that have not yet been implemented or finalized.

## 1. Ideas from Developer Brainstorming (Feb 25, 2026)

### Audio-Responsive Gameplay Mechanics
*   **Buffs Altering Audio:** Tower buffs could modify sound effects while active (e.g., increasing fire rate pitches up the sound, or adds reverb/distortion).
*   **Visual Feedback on Pads:** Using buffs could manipulate the surrounding environment to some degree (e.g., changing the appearance or pulsing of the "grey blocks/cubes" or the background visualizer pads).


### Relic Placement Mechanics
*   **Physical Relic Slotting:** Instead of just a UI Loadout, relics could physical slot into the environment (the "grey cubes" or sound pads).
*   **Slot Constraints:** Limit to 3-5 designated slots on the sound pads where players must choose to place their relics.
*   **Visual Representation:** Relics have a physical presence with cool designs that pulse, visually representing their effects on the board alongside their gameplay buffs.

### Maze Modifiers (Healing Zones)
*   **Environmental Healing:** Introduce sections of the maze where enemies regenerate health.
*   **Strategic Placement Challenge:** Forces players to decide whether to place towers in front of the healing zone to burn them down early, or pack everything behind the zone and deal with a larger, fully-healed wave.
*   **Secondary Penalty for Ignoring:** If a player places zero towers *before* reaching the healing zone, the mobs gain an additional speed buff along with their health regeneration.

## 2. Outstanding Design Questions (from Game Brief)

This section contains existing open questions from previous design sessions that remain unresolved.

### Economy & Progression
*   **AP Growth:** How does the player's maximum Allocation Points (AP) increase? Is it a fixed progression per stage or a separate upgrade currency?
*   **Unlock Economy:** What is the full mapping of what unlocks where across the 10 stages (towers, buffs, relics, AP)?

### Tower & Upgrades
*   **Upgrade Branching:** Can the player mix upgrade choices across tiers (e.g., Tier 1A + Tier 2B), or must they commit to a single linear path?
*   **Relic Design:** Specific passive and active ability definitions for each individual relic.
*   **Buff Design:** Specific effect impacts, gold costs, and cooldown values.

### Enemy & Level Design
*   **Enemy Variants:** Detailed designs for shielded, resistant, and other audio-themed waveform variations.
*   **Difficulty Scaling:** How exactly does the difficulty ramp up across stages and within specific stem levels?
*   **Final Stage Mechanics:** Does the custom song selection entirely dictate gameplay mechanics on the final stage, or is the combination of stems purely aesthetic?

### Artistic Identity
*   **Music Source:** Will the music be original compositions, licensed tracks, or procedurally generated?
*   **Game Title:** Finalize the official name for the project (currently "TD-Prototype").
